# 🐾 ShelPet

**ShelPet** is a modern animal welfare and pet management platform designed to connect rescuers, adopters, pet lovers, and pet businesses in one secure ecosystem. The platform simplifies animal rescue, responsible adoption, and pet-related services while ensuring user authenticity through an integrated verification system.

---

## 📖 Overview

ShelPet aims to reduce the number of abandoned and homeless animals by providing a trusted digital platform where users can:

* Rescue animals and request emergency assistance
* Find loving homes through responsible adoption
* Earn through paid fostering and adoption services
* Purchase pet supplies from verified pet shops
* Communicate securely with other users
* Receive real-time notifications
* Build trust with NID verification

The platform focuses on transparency, safety, and convenience for everyone involved in animal welfare.
